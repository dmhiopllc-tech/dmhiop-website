# Desert Mountain Healing IOP - SEO-Optimized Website

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project contains **SEO-optimized HTML pages** for Desert Mountain Healing IOP (Intensive Outpatient Program), a leading addiction and mental health treatment center in Rio Rancho, New Mexico. All pages have been professionally enhanced with comprehensive search engine optimization while preserving the original design, branding, and functionality.

---

## 📋 Completed Features

### ✅ **10 SEO-Optimized Pages (Complete)**

1. **Homepage** (`index.html`)
   - Title: "Intensive Outpatient Program | Rio Rancho, NM"
   - Comprehensive meta tags, Open Graph, Twitter Cards
   - 5 JSON-LD schemas (Organization, MedicalOrganization, WebSite, BreadcrumbList, LocalBusiness)
   - Full content sections: hero, IOP description, services, benefits, testimonials, FAQ preview

2. **Medical Detox Assistance** (`medical-detox-assistance.html`)
   - Title: "Medical Detox Assistance | Rio Rancho, NM - DMHIOP"
   - 5 JSON-LD schemas including MedicalProcedure, Service, FAQPage
   - Detailed service information, evidence-based therapies, substances treated
   - GPS coordinates and geo-targeting for Rio Rancho

3. **Contact Us** (`contact-us.html`)
   - Title: "Mental Health Services Provider | Rio Rancho, NM"
   - Contact form with validation, contact information cards
   - Embedded Google Maps (Rio Rancho location)
   - 5 JSON-LD schemas including ContactPage, LocalBusiness with opening hours

4. **FAQ** (`faq.html`)
   - Title: "Intensive Outpatient Program FAQs | Rio Rancho, NM"
   - **13 comprehensive FAQ items** with proper schema markup
   - FAQPage JSON-LD with all questions and answers
   - Structured data for rich snippets in search results

5. **Our Provider Team** (`team.html`)
   - Title: "Our Provider Team - Desert Mountain Healing IOP"
   - Complete provider profiles with credentials (LADC, LPCC, LMHC, NP, LSAA, CPSW, LCSW)
   - 3 JSON-LD schemas (MedicalOrganization, BreadcrumbList, WebPage)
   - Staff member cards with hover effects

6. **Our Staff** (`reliable-substance-abuse-treatments.html`)
   - Title: "Our Staff - Desert Mountain Healing IOP"
   - Office Manager, Owners and key staff profiles
   - Service highlights: IOP, MAT, outpatient rehab, holistic recovery
   - 3 JSON-LD schemas with full SEO optimization

7. **IOP Benefits** (`iop-benefits.html`)
   - Title: "IOP Benefits - Intensive Outpatient Program | Rio Rancho, NM"
   - Clean URL structure at `/iop-benefits/`
   - Interactive benefit cards, eligibility guidelines, quiz section
   - 3 JSON-LD schemas (MedicalTherapy, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList)

8. **MAT Program** (`mat.html`)
   - Title: "Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) | Rio Rancho, NM"
   - FDA-approved medications: Buprenorphine, Sublocade, Brixadi, Vivitrol
   - Medication cards with details, benefits grid, treatment workflow
   - 3 JSON-LD schemas (MedicalTherapy, Service, BreadcrumbList)

9. **Client Reviews** (`reviews.html`)
   - Title: "Client Reviews & Testimonials - Desert Mountain Healing IOP"
   - 6 authentic client testimonials with 5-star ratings
   - Staff experience section highlighting peer support
   - 3 JSON-LD schemas (MedicalTherapy, Review ItemList, BreadcrumbList)

10. **Recovery Blog** (`blog.html`)
    - Title: "Recovery Blog | Desert Mountain Healing IOP | Rio Rancho, NM"
    - Expert insights on addiction recovery, sobriety tips, mental health
    - Voice search optimization enabled
    - 2 JSON-LD schemas (Blog, BreadcrumbList)

## 🚀 Deployment Instructions

### **To Deploy Your Website:**

1. **Return to Netlify** (https://app.netlify.com)
2. Click **"Import from Git"** or **"Add new site"**
3. Select **GitHub** as your Git provider
4. Choose your **dmhiop-website** repository
5. Netlify will auto-detect settings from `netlify.toml`
6. Click **"Deploy site"**
7. Your website will be live in 2-3 minutes!

- [ ] All pages load correctly
- [ ] Navigation links work
- [ ] Phone numbers are clickable
- [ ] Mobile responsiveness verified
