IOP Benefits

Flexible, effective treatment that fits your life while supporting lasting recovery

Why Choose Intensive Outpatient Treatment?

Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) offer a unique balance between comprehensive treatment and the flexibility to maintain your daily responsibilities. At Desert Mountain Healing IOP, our evidence-based program provides the support you need while allowing you to stay connected to your family, work, and community.

Flexibility & Independence

Continue working, attending school, or caring for family while receiving treatment. Our flexible scheduling options include daytime and evening sessions to accommodate your life.

Evidence-Based Treatment

Receive comprehensive care using proven therapeutic approaches including CBT, DBT, and motivational interviewing, all delivered by licensed, experienced providers.

Cost-Effective Care

IOP provides intensive treatment at a fraction of the cost of residential programs, and most insurance plans cover IOP services. We accept major insurance providers and offer affordable self-pay options.

Community Support

Build meaningful connections with peers facing similar challenges in a supportive group environment. Our community-based approach fosters lasting recovery relationships.

Real-World Application

Practice recovery skills in your everyday environment, addressing real-life triggers and challenges with immediate professional support and guidance.

Family Involvement

Stay connected with loved ones throughout treatment. We offer family education and support to strengthen your recovery network and improve family dynamics.

Comprehensive IOP Advantages

Structured Yet Flexible Schedule

Our IOP typically meets 3-5 days per week for 3 hours per session, providing structured support while allowing you to maintain your daily routine. Sessions are available during the day and evening to accommodate work and family schedules.

  • Morning, afternoon, and evening session options
  • Consistent schedule for accountability
  • Ability to continue employment or education
  • Time to practice recovery skills in real-world settings

Clinical Excellence & Personalized Care

Our licensed clinical team creates individualized treatment plans tailored to your specific needs, goals, and circumstances. You'll receive comprehensive assessments and ongoing progress monitoring.

  • Individualized treatment planning
  • Licensed, experienced providers (LADC, LPCC, LCSW, NP)
  • Evidence-based therapeutic modalities
  • Medication management when appropriate
  • Regular progress assessments and treatment adjustments

Holistic Treatment Approach

We address not just substance use or mental health symptoms, but the whole person—mind, body, and spirit. Our integrated approach considers all aspects of your wellbeing.

  • Mental health and substance abuse co-occurring treatment
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) options
  • Life skills development
  • Relapse prevention strategies
  • Wellness and self-care education

Smooth Transition of Care

IOP serves as an ideal step-down from residential or inpatient treatment, or as a primary intervention for those who don't require 24-hour care. We provide continuity as you progress through levels of care.

  • Step-down from higher levels of care
  • Bridge to outpatient therapy
  • Aftercare planning and support
  • Coordination with other providers

Insurance Coverage & Affordability

Most major insurance plans cover IOP services, making comprehensive treatment accessible and affordable. Our team handles insurance verification and billing to minimize your stress.

  • Accepted by major insurance providers
  • Free insurance verification
  • Affordable self-pay options
  • Sliding scale fees available
  • Payment plans when needed
