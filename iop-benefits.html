IOP Benefits in Rio Rancho, NM

Comprehensive addiction treatment that fits your life

Receive the support you need at Desert Mountain Healing IOP. Our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) in Rio Rancho, NM, provides comprehensive addiction and dual diagnosis treatment for those in the Albuquerque metro area. This structured program offers the support and guidance of an outpatient rehab, allowing you to get the help you need while managing your daily life, work, or school.

Guidelines

You may qualify for our IOP benefits in Rio Rancho, NM, if you meet the following criteria:

  • Struggle with substance use or co-occurring mental health issues but don't require inpatient care.
  • Have completed detox or a residential program and need continued structured support.
  • Prefer a flexible dual diagnosis treatment option that allows you to continue working, attending school, or managing daily life.
  • Need a supportive recovery environment with group therapy and professional counseling.
  • Have experienced relapse and need a structured program to regain stability.

Quick Self-Assessment Quiz

If you're still unsure if our IOP treatment and relapse prevention planning is ideal for you, ask yourself the following questions:

  • Have I tried quitting or cutting back on substances but struggled to stay sober?
  • Do I feel my substance use is affecting my work, relationships, or mental health?
  • Have I recently completed detox or inpatient treatment and need continued support?
  • Do I experience cravings, anxiety, depression, or emotional struggles related to substance use?
  • Am I looking for a program that offers structure, accountability, and flexibility?

If you answered "Yes" to one or more of these questions, our IOP for addiction recovery program could be the right fit for you.
