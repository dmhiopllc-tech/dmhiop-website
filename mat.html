Commonly Used Medications in MAT

Our program utilizes the following FDA-approved medications:

Buprenorphine (Suboxone®)

This medication significantly reduces opioid cravings and withdrawal symptoms, helping individuals stabilize during their recovery journey.

SUBLOCADE

Similar to Buprenorphine, it helps manage opioid cravings and withdrawal symptoms, fostering a stable recovery environment.

Brixadi

It blocks the euphoric effects of opioids, thereby decreasing the risk of relapse.

VIVITROL

An injectable medicine specifically used to treat alcohol dependence. It is crucial to abstain from drinking before beginning VIVITROL treatment.
