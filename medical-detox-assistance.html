Our Outpatient Detox Services

Our outpatient medical detox assistance program is designed for individuals who need structured support during the detoxification process and have been assessed by our Nurse Practitioner as medically stable enough for outpatient care. As an outpatient program, you'll attend scheduled sessions while living at home. We provide:

  • Medical Assessment by Our Nurse Practitioner: Comprehensive evaluation to determine if you are medically stable for outpatient detox support
  • Withdrawal Management: Support and guidance through the withdrawal process
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): FDA-approved medications when appropriate
  • Individual Counseling: One-on-one sessions with licensed therapists
  • Group Therapy: Peer support in a safe, structured environment
  • Family Support: Education and guidance for loved ones
  • Care Coordination: Connection to medical providers and community resources
  • Aftercare Planning: Comprehensive discharge planning for continued success

Is Outpatient Detox Right for You?

Our outpatient medical detox assistance program may be appropriate for individuals who meet the following criteria and are deemed medically stable by our Nurse Practitioner. Remember, this is an outpatient program - you will not be staying at our facility:

  • Are experiencing mild to moderate withdrawal symptoms
  • Have a stable living environment and strong support system
  • Are motivated to engage in treatment
  • Do not have severe co-occurring medical or psychiatric conditions
  • Can commit to attending regular treatment sessions
  • Prefer the flexibility of an outpatient program

Important Note: Detoxification from alcohol or certain drugs can be dangerous without proper medical supervision. If you're experiencing severe withdrawal symptoms, seizures, hallucinations, or other medical emergencies, please seek immediate medical attention at an emergency room or call 911. Our Nurse Practitioner will assess if outpatient detox is safe and appropriate for your situation. For those requiring inpatient detoxification with 24/7 medical monitoring, we will help coordinate care with appropriate facilities.

What to Expect

1. Initial Assessment

Your journey begins with a comprehensive assessment. We'll evaluate your medical history, substance use patterns, mental health, and support system to create a personalized treatment plan.

2. Treatment Planning

Based on your assessment, we'll develop a customized plan that may include individual therapy, group sessions, medication management, and other supportive services.

3. Active Outpatient Treatment

During the outpatient detox phase, you'll attend scheduled sessions at our facility while living at home. You'll receive regular monitoring and support during your visits. We'll help you manage withdrawal symptoms safely while beginning the therapeutic work that supports long-term recovery. You will not be staying at our facility - this is an outpatient program.

4. Transition to IOP

Once you've completed the detox phase, many clients transition to our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) for continued care. Our IOP provides structured therapy and support as you build the skills needed for lasting sobriety.

5. Aftercare Planning

Before completing treatment, we'll work with you to develop a comprehensive aftercare plan. This includes connections to support groups, ongoing therapy, and other community resources.
