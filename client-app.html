Everything You Need to Support Your Recovery

Own Today provides powerful tools to help you stay engaged, motivated, and connected throughout your treatment journey.

📍

Meeting Guide

Find support meetings near you with geofence check-ins. Access listings for AA, NA, LifeRing, SMART Recovery, and Celebrate Recovery meetings. Earn points for every check-in!

📊

Progress Tracking

Track your daily streaks, meeting attendance, and earn points for every positive action. Watch your momentum build day by day!

✍️

Private Journaling

Your story matters. Write freely in your secure journal to reflect on your journey and track your emotional growth over time.

🎯

Goal Setting

Set meaningful goals and celebrate each achievement. Break down big dreams into actionable steps and track your progress.

🧘

Guided Meditation

Access guided meditation sessions anytime for stress relief, mindfulness, and inner peace. Find your calm when you need it most.

💬

24/7 AI Support

Never feel alone. Our AI support chat is available around the clock when you need someone to talk to or guidance in a moment of need.

🏆

Rewards System

Earn points for every positive action and redeem them for meaningful rewards. Celebrate your wins and stay motivated!

📅

Appointment Management

Never miss a session. Manage your therapy appointments, check-ins, and important dates all in one place.

🤝

Community Connection

Connect with others on the same journey. Share experiences, offer support, and build meaningful relationships in recovery.

🎉

Sobriety Calculator

Count every victory! Track your sobriety milestones and celebrate each day of progress on your recovery journey.

📖

Literature Library

Access recovery resources, daily meditations, and inspirational readings. Knowledge is power on your healing path.

💫

Daily Affirmations

Start each day with positive affirmations designed to inspire, encourage, and motivate you toward continued growth.

🛡️

Relapse Prevention

Access proven tools and strategies to help you stay strong when things get tough. Build your resilience toolkit.
