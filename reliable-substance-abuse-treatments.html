Comprehensive Treatment Services in Rio Rancho, NM

Evidence-based addiction and mental health care serving the Albuquerque metro area

Expert Care for Lasting Recovery

At Desert Mountain Healing IOP, we provide a full spectrum of addiction treatment and mental health services designed to support you at every stage of your recovery journey. From initial assessment to aftercare planning, our comprehensive programs are tailored to meet your unique needs.

Located in Rio Rancho and proudly serving the greater Albuquerque metropolitan area, we combine evidence-based practices with compassionate, client-centered care to help you achieve lasting sobriety and improved well-being.

🏥

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Our flagship program provides structured therapy sessions 3-5 days per week, including individual counseling, group therapy, and family support. Designed to fit your schedule while delivering comprehensive treatment.

Includes:

  • Individual therapy sessions
  • Group therapy & peer support
  • Family counseling
  • Relapse prevention planning
  • Aftercare coordination

Medication Assisted Treatment (MAT)

Evidence-based approach combining FDA-approved medications with counseling and behavioral therapies to treat substance use disorders. MAT has been proven to improve treatment outcomes and support long-term recovery.

We offer:

  • Buprenorphine (Suboxone)
  • SUBLOCADE injections
  • Comprehensive medication management
  • Ongoing counseling support
  • Coordinated care with providers
Learn More About MAT →
🩺

Medical Detox Assistance

Safe, medically supervised outpatient detoxification support to help you start your recovery journey. Our Nurse Practitioner conducts thorough assessments to determine if outpatient detox is appropriate for your situation.

Our approach:

  • NP medical assessment
  • Outpatient detox support
  • Medication management
  • Regular monitoring
  • Transition to ongoing treatment

Specialized treatment for:
