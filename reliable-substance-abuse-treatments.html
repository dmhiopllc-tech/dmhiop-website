Our IOP Medical Staff in Rio Rancho, NM

Compassionate professionals dedicated to your recovery journey

Be part of our intensive outpatient substance abuse treatment program. At Desert Mountain Healing IOP, our compassionate and dedicated IOP medical staff in Rio Rancho, NM, creates a welcoming and supportive environment for our clients, regardless of their background. From the moment you walk through our doors, our team ensures a seamless experience by assisting with scheduling, coordinating insurance, and answering your questions.

Our licensed addiction specialists and medical clinicians are committed to fostering a space where individuals feel valued and understood. This dedication to care and efficiency allows them to focus on providing high-quality substance abuse treatment, including outpatient rehab, medication-assisted treatment (MAT), and holistic recovery approaches.

Our Comprehensive Support Services Include:

  • Scheduling assistance and appointment coordination
  • Insurance verification and coordination
  • Personalized treatment planning
  • Medication-assisted treatment (MAT)
  • Outpatient rehab programs
  • Holistic recovery approaches
  • Administrative support throughout your journey
