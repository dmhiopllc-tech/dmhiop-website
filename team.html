Meet Our Expert Provider Team

We are committed to providing compassionate and effective care for individuals navigating mental health challenges and substance use recovery. As a leading community mental health provider in Rio Rancho, NM, our team has specialized expertise in evidence-based treatment approaches, clinical leadership, and patient-centered care. They play a unique role in ensuring clients receive the highest level of support while working toward emotional stability, sobriety, and long-term well-being.

Our providers come from diverse clinical and personal backgrounds, uniting under the shared goal of helping individuals and families build resilience, restore hope, and achieve meaningful recovery. If you or a loved one is seeking a trusted partner in healing, Desert Mountain Healing IOP offers the compassionate support and evidence-based care you need.

Robert Galligan

LADC, LPCC

Clinical Director

As the Clinical Director, Robert Galligan brings strong leadership and dedication to our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP). A Licensed Alcohol and Drug Counselor (LADC) and Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor (LPCC), Robert has years of experience in guiding individuals through complex mental health concerns. He is responsible for overseeing the clinical operations of the program while ensuring every client receives personalized, effective care.

Robert's expertise includes trauma, anxiety, and depression treatment, along with program development and clinical supervision. He provides a client-centered approach that blends professional insight with compassionate support. His leadership allows the entire team to deliver consistent, high-quality services that empower individuals in the Rio Rancho and Albuquerque metro area to overcome obstacles and achieve healthier, more balanced lives.

Paula Eberhardt

LADAC

Licensed Alcohol and Drug Abuse Counselor

As a Licensed Alcohol and Drug Abuse Counselor (LADAC), Paula Eberhardt brings dedicated expertise in addiction treatment and recovery support. She specializes in helping individuals navigate the challenges of substance use disorders through evidence-based therapeutic approaches tailored to each client's unique needs.

Paula is passionate about empowering clients to build healthier coping strategies and achieve lasting sobriety. Her compassionate, client-centered approach creates a safe and supportive environment where individuals feel heard, understood, and motivated to take meaningful steps toward recovery. Paula's commitment to professional excellence and genuine care makes her an invaluable member of the Desert Mountain Healing IOP team.

Jennifer Barela

NP

Certified Nurse Practitioner

As a Certified Nurse Practitioner (NP), Jennifer Barela plays a vital role in the success of our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP). She has medical expertise and offers patient-centered care that supports individuals on their journey toward emotional and physical wellness.

Jennifer specializes in conducting thorough medical assessments, managing medications, and collaborating with the clinical team to deliver well-rounded mental health services. Her ability to blend clinical precision with compassionate communication helps clients feel supported as they address both substance use and mental health challenges. Through her commitment to individualized care, she ensures every client has the resources needed to achieve lasting recovery.

Her client-centered approach is designed to help individuals establish realistic goals while learning new strategies for long-term recovery.

Regina excels at developing personalized treatment plans that reflect each client's unique circumstances. She provides evidence-based tools and ongoing support to help individuals achieve stability and improved emotional well-being. Her commitment to offering compassionate care makes her an integral member of the Desert Mountain Healing IOP team.

Adam Brue

LSAA

Michelle Martin

CPSW

Certified Peer Support Worker

Michelle Martin serves as a Certified Peer Support Worker (CPSW) at Desert Mountain Healing IOP. Drawing from her personal experience and professional training, she provides practical support and encouragement to those navigating substance use recovery.
