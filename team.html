Meet Our Team

Expert providers and dedicated administrative staff committed to your recovery

Our Providers

We are committed to providing compassionate and effective care for individuals navigating mental health challenges and substance use recovery. As a leading community mental health provider in Rio Rancho, NM, our team has specialized expertise in evidence-based treatment approaches, clinical leadership, and patient-centered care. They play a unique role in ensuring clients receive the highest level of support while working toward emotional stability, sobriety, and long-term well-being.

Our providers come from diverse clinical and personal backgrounds, uniting under the shared goal of helping individuals and families build resilience, restore hope, and achieve meaningful recovery. If you or a loved one is seeking a trusted partner in healing, Desert Mountain Healing IOP offers the compassionate support and evidence-based care you need.

Robert Galligan

LADC, LPCC

Clinical Director

As the Clinical Director, Robert Galligan brings strong leadership and dedication to our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP). A Licensed Alcohol and Drug Counselor (LADC) and Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor (LPCC), Robert has years of experience in guiding individuals through complex mental health concerns. He is responsible for overseeing the clinical operations of the program while ensuring every client receives personalized, effective care.

Robert's expertise includes trauma, anxiety, and depression treatment, along with program development and clinical supervision. He provides a client-centered approach that blends professional insight with compassionate support. His leadership allows the entire team to deliver consistent, high-quality services that empower individuals in the Rio Rancho and Albuquerque metro area to overcome obstacles and achieve healthier, more balanced lives.
