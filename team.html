What Is an Intensive Outpatient Program?

An Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) is a structured treatment program designed for individuals struggling with substance abuse or mental health challenges who require more support than traditional outpatient care but don't need 24-hour supervision.

At Desert Mountain Healing, our IOP provides flexible scheduling, evidence-based therapies, and a supportive community environment that allows you to maintain your daily responsibilities while receiving comprehensive treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does IOP treatment cost?

Treatment costs vary based on individual needs and insurance coverage. We work with most major insurance providers and offer flexible payment options. Contact us for a free insurance verification.

How long does the IOP program last?

IOP programs typically last 6-12 weeks, depending on individual progress and treatment goals. Your care team will work with you to create a personalized treatment plan.

Can I work while attending IOP?

Yes! Our IOP is designed to be flexible, allowing you to maintain employment and family responsibilities while receiving comprehensive treatment. We offer morning, afternoon, and evening sessions.
