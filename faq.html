Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our intensive outpatient program in Rio Rancho, NM

If you have questions about our addiction treatment options, explore our FAQs to learn how our intensive outpatient program can support your recovery journey in Rio Rancho, NM.

What is Desert Mountain Healing IOP?

Desert Mountain Healing IOP is an in-person intensive outpatient program and substance use treatment program in Rio Rancho, NM. We provide structured, evidence-based treatment for individuals struggling with substance use disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions. Our outpatient rehab is designed to help you regain control of your life.

Where is Desert Mountain Healing IOP located?

We are conveniently located in Rio Rancho, New Mexico, proudly serving the entire Albuquerque metro area. Please call us for our exact address and directions to our facility.

What makes your program different from others?

Our program is a top choice for addiction treatment because we offer:

  • In-Person Treatment – Receive real support and accountability in a dedicated, healing environment.
  • Dual Diagnosis Care – We treat both addiction and mental health conditions simultaneously for lasting recovery.
  • Flexible Scheduling – Our IOP is designed to fit your life, allowing you to maintain daily responsibilities.
  • Experienced, Compassionate Team – Our licensed medical professionals in Rio Rancho, NM, are dedicated to your recovery journey.

What is an Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)?

An IOP is a structured treatment program that allows individuals to receive comprehensive therapy and support while living at home. Our program includes individual therapy, group therapy, relapse prevention planning, and life skills training.

How long does the IOP program last?

The length of our IOP varies depending on individual needs. Most participants attend for 8-12 weeks, with sessions multiple times per week.

What therapies are included in the program?

We offer a variety of evidence-based therapies, including:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Motivational Interviewing (MI)
  • Group Therapy & Peer Support
  • Relapse Prevention & Life Skills

Do you offer family support?

Yes! Family involvement is encouraged, and we provide education and support resources to help loved ones understand addiction and recovery.

Do you accept insurance?

Absolutely! We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicaid and commercial providers. Contact us to verify your coverage.

How can I verify my insurance coverage?

You can call us directly. Our team will review your benefits and help you understand your coverage options.

How do I enroll in the program?

Call us for a confidential consultation. You'll complete an assessment to determine the best treatment plan. Begin your journey to recovery with personalized support.

Is there a waitlist?

Availability depends on demand, but we strive to offer prompt admissions. Contact us to check current openings.

Can I work or go to school while in IOP?

Yes! Our flexible scheduling allows you to continue daily responsibilities while receiving treatment.

What should I bring to my first session?

Please bring:

  • ✔ A Valid ID
  • ✔ Insurance Information
  • ✔ A List of Current Medications
  • ✔ A Willingness to Heal and Grow
