About Our Intensive Outpatient Program

Desert Mountain Healing IOP provides compassionate, evidence-based treatment for substance abuse and co-occurring disorders. Our flexible program allows you to maintain work, school, and family commitments while receiving the comprehensive care you need for lasting recovery.

Our Services

Intensive Outpatient Program

Structured treatment sessions 3-5 days per week while maintaining your daily responsibilities. Evidence-based therapies in a supportive environment.

Medical Detox Assistance

Safe, supervised detoxification with medical support to manage withdrawal symptoms and prepare for treatment.

Medication-Assisted Treatment

FDA-approved medications combined with counseling for effective treatment of opioid and alcohol use disorders.

Comprehensive Treatment

Individual therapy, group counseling, family support, and holistic approaches tailored to your unique needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the IOP program cost?

We accept most major insurance plans. Our team will work with you to verify your benefits and explore payment options to make treatment affordable.

How long is the program?

Program length varies based on individual needs, typically ranging from 8-12 weeks. Your treatment plan will be customized to support your recovery goals.

Can I work while in the program?

Yes! Our intensive outpatient program is designed to accommodate work, school, and family commitments with flexible scheduling options.
